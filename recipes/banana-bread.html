Banana Bread Recipe

Photo taken by Cindy Kerr on AllRecipes

Description

Banana Bread that is guaranteed to taste delicious and sweet.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz butter
  • 6 oz sugar
  • 6 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. Add sugar. Mix together.
  3. Use mixer to mix out bananas (or mix by hand with spoon).
  4. Melt butter.
  5. Beat eggs and add vanilla.
  6. Mix together and pour into melted butter.
  7. Mix, then pour into crushed bananas.
  8. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients.
  9. Add mixture and fold in [Do not overmix].
  10. Pour into loaf pan.
  11. Bake at 350oF for 45 minutes.
