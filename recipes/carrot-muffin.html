Carrot Muffin Recipe

Photo taken by Lisa Barlow on AllRecipes

Description

Irresistible and delicious carrot muffins are the guarantee of this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz butter
  • 6 oz sugar
  • 5 - 6 carrots
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, and baking soda.
  2. Add sugar. Mix together.
  3. Grate/ Shred carrots.
  4. Melt butter. Beat eggs and add vanilla.
  5. Mix together and pour into the melted butter.
  6. Mix, then fold in carrots.
  7. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients.
  8. Add mixture and fold in [Do not overmix].
  9. Pour ingredients in muffin tin [Do not fill the cups too much].
  10. Bake at 350oF for 45 minutes.
