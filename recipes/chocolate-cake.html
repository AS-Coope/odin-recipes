Chocolate Cake Recipe

Photo taken by christineandwilly

Description

Mouth-watering, delicious and tasty chocolate cake will be yours in following this recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 oz margarine
  • 6 tbsp cocoa powder [5 tbsp cocoa + 1 tbsp coffee]
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cups flour [cake and pastry]
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda [baking soda]
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Put cocoa powder, oil, margarine and water into a pot. Place on fire and melt [Mix while melting]. Put to cool.
  2. Sift flour and baking soda. Add sugar and mix together.
  3. Pour sour milk into container.
  4. Add eggs one by one; add vanilla and whisk.
  5. Pour into chocolate mixture and mix.
  6. Make a hole in the middle of dry mixture.
  7. Pour in liquid and mix [Do not overmix].
  8. Pour into greased and floured baking tin.
  9. Bake for ≈ 50 mins at 350oF or until done.

Tips

  1. Can substitute 1 tbsp coffee for 1 tbsp cocoa for heightened flavour. Also, add a little rum (removes rawness of eggs).
  2. Make sour milk by adding about 2 capsful of lime juice to 1/2 cup milk.
